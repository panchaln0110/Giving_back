Giving Back

Giving back is part of our DNA

ACN’s vision for a better existence extends way beyond their business practices.

It’s crucial to communities, too. From the very beginning, ACN wanted to include philanthropy as a critical element to its business model. How could the company create a program every customer could help with when they support an ACN Independent Business Owner?

Enter ACN’s Project Feeding Kids. Together with Feeding America and Food Banks Canada, ACN works toward a single mission: To fight childhood hunger while empowering families to live healthier lives.

When a consumer pays for a service most can’t imagine living without, they provide a meal for a child who needs it most.

Every customer. Every bill. Every time.

It’s simple, yet powerful.

ACN’s philanthropic interests don’t stop there.

We especially like to contribute locally and support communities in which our IBOs live and work.

Ronald McDonald House

ACN is proud to support Ronald McDonald House Charities, which seeks to provide a home away from home for families when a loved one must travel for medical care.

Families won’t have to worry about hot meals, comfortable living spaces, laundry facilities and more while they’re here with their critically ill or injured children. ACN, its employees and Independent Business Owners volunteer and donate millions of dollars worldwide. In fact, at each of ACN’s International Events worldwide, ACN raises funds for the local chapter of the House and then matches those donations dollar for dollar, presenting a check to the charity at the close of the event.
